About

I'm a third year computer science student at Northeastern University. I work hard and make things.

In my free time, I like to hack on my own projects. I open source all of my code, and you can check it out on Github! On Thursday nights you can find me at NU Hacks hanging out with friends, talking about tech, and generally not getting anything accomplished.

I am active on the English Wikipedia, where I'm an administrator and arbitrator. I occasionally write articles, but spend more of my time reverting vandalism, resolving disputes, and generally trying to do my part to keep the whole thing from falling apart. When I need a break from that, I edit the English Wikisource, where I'm currently transcribing the Pentagon Papers.
